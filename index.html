Block grid

Block grids give you a way to evenly split contents of a list within the grid. If you wanted to create a row of five images or paragraphs that need to stay evenly spaced no matter the screen size, the block grid is for you.

Pagination

Pagination is a type of navigation that lets users tap through a series of related pages. Moving between pages has become less common with the advent of longer pages and AJAX loading, but if you need pagination, Foundation has you covered.
